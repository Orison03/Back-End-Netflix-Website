What service gives free Netflix?

Netflix on Us allows qualifying T-Mobile customers to watch free movies online on one of the best streaming services. T-Mobile's Magenta with two or more lines or Magenta MAX with a single line gets you the Netflix Basic plan, with support for standard-definition streaming on a single screen..

As of September 2022, Netflix had 222 million subscribers worldwide, including 73.3 million in the United States and Canada; 73.0 million in Europe, the Middle East and Africa, 39.6 million in Latin America and 34.8 million in the Asia-Pacific region.[12] It is available worldwide aside from Mainland China, Syria, North Korea, and Russia. Netflix has played a prominent role in independent film distribution, and it is a member of the Motion Picture Association (MPA).

Netflix can be accessed via web browsers or via application software installed on smart TVs, set-top boxes connected to televisions, tablet computers, smartphones, digital media players, Blu-ray players, video game consoles and virtual reality headsets on the list of Netflix-compatible devices.[13][14][15][16] It is available in 4K resolution.[17] In the United States, the company provided Digital Video Disc (DVD)[18] and Blu-ray rentals delivered individually via the United States Postal Service from regional warehouses.
